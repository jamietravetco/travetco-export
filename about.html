1/5 Veterinary Medicine Company has become one of leading veterinary medicine companies in the Mekong Delta achieving certificates of production process Powder Medicine, Injectable Medicine and Oral Medicine.
With the wide distribution network over the country, the company’s products diversify and meet the customer’s demand in the market for products of diseases prevention and treatment for cattle, poultry and aquaculture.

We were established in 1998, specializing in manufacturing and trading veterinary pharmaceutical products which meet the international standard.
Situated in Tra Noc 2 Industrial Zone of Can Tho City, the factory has modern equipment and machinery for production, inspection, and development of products with no-ceasing.
